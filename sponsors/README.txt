SPONSOR LOGOS GO IN THIS FOLDER
================================
1. Upload each sponsor's logo here (PNG with transparent background works
   best; roughly 400px wide is plenty).
2. In your Google Sheet, open the "Sponsors" tab and add a row per sponsor:
     sponsorName | logoFile      | websiteUrl              | tier
     ECAC        | ecac.png      | https://eauclaire...    | title
     Bow Physio  | bowphysio.png | https://...             |
   - logoFile must exactly match the file name uploaded here (case matters).
   - Leave logoFile blank to show the sponsor's name as styled text instead.
   - tier "title" floats that sponsor to the top with a Title Sponsor tag
     and adds "Presented by ..." to the app header. Leave blank for standard.
3. That's it - the app updates on next load. Empty tab = no sponsor cards
   appear anywhere.
